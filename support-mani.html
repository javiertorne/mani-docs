‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

How do I sync my data across devices?

Mani automatically syncs your data with iCloud if you have it enabled. Make sure to:

  • Be signed in with the same Apple ID on all your devices
  • Have iCloud enabled in Settings > [Your name] > iCloud
  • Have an internet connection

How do I cancel my PRO subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through Apple:

  1. Go to Settings on your iOS/macOS device
  2. Tap your name at the top
  3. Select "Subscriptions"
  4. Find "Mani PRO" and tap "Cancel Subscription"

You can also manage your subscriptions on Apple's website.

Is my data safe?

Yes. All your financial data is stored only on your device and in your personal iCloud. We do not have access to your information. Read more in our Privacy Policy.

Can I export my data?

We currently don't offer a data export feature. This functionality is on our list of future improvements. Meanwhile, your data is available on all your Apple devices through iCloud.

I found a bug, how do I report it?

Thank you for helping us improve! Send us an email at developer@makotostudio.dev with:

  • Description of the problem
  • Steps to reproduce it
  • iOS/macOS version and app version
  • Screenshots (if possible)

How do I delete my account and all my data?

Mani doesn't require a user account. To delete all your data:

  1. Uninstall the app from all your devices
  2. Go to Settings > [Your name] > iCloud > Manage Storage
  3. Find "Mani" and delete the data
